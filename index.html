FRAGMENTS OF NEW YORK

Architecture • Texture • Urban Rhythm

A visual study of New York City architecture, focusing on repetition, ornament, cast-iron details, and the quiet rhythm of urban structures.

Repetition and symmetry across a historic New York facade.

Soft architectural details shaped by light, texture, and structure.

Vertical rhythm created through windows, columns, and ornament.

Decorative ironwork and layered historic detail in downtown Manhattan.

Geometry and repetition within a red brick urban facade.

Surface detail as a record of material, history, and design.

Scale and weight expressed through stone, arches, and shadow.

Gothic forms emerging through vertical lines and carved detail.

Ornament and structure meet through repeated carved patterns.

Figures embedded into architecture, turning facade into narrative.

About

I’m Prachi Patel, a New York–based creative exploring architecture, texture, and visual storytelling through photography and design. My work focuses on details, repetition, and atmosphere throughout NYC.
