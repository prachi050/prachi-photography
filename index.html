Process

My process begins with observation: walking, framing, cropping, and identifying repeated visual systems in the city.

Observation

I look for rhythm in windows, facades, shadows, and ornament throughout the city.

Composition

I use cropping, repetition, and negative space to turn architecture into structured visual studies.

Design Connection

My photography connects to UI/UX and graphic design through hierarchy, systems, and visual storytelling.

About

I’m Prachi Patel, a New York-based designer and photographer exploring the relationship between architecture, visual systems, and digital experience. My background in computer science, graphic design, and photography informs my interest in interaction, structure, rhythm, and urban observation. Currently completing a B.A. in Computer Science with a minor in Graphic Design at Pace University in New York City.
